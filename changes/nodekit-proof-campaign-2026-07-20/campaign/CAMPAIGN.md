# NodeKit launches NodeKit — campaign contract

This campaign is a recursive dogfood run, not a permissionless social bot.
NodeKit may research, draft, render, upload, publish, and verify the exact
authorized campaign. It may not reply, direct-message, follow, vote, buy
promotion, or broaden the publication surface.

## Public thesis

Most coding agents stop when code exists. NodeKit carries one bounded product
journey through research, generation, deterministic evaluation, live-provider
testing, browser QA, deployment, presentation, distribution, and proof.

## Reality anchors

- exact Git commits and config/application hashes;
- public GitHub repositories and release tags;
- strict provider receipts, including blocked attempts;
- fresh-browser screenshots and traces;
- exact deployment identity;
- exported and reopened artifacts;
- public post URLs and screenshots.

## Tense gate

Only claims with `verified`, `measured`, or `observed` status in
`../story/claims.json` may appear in past tense. `planned` claims must remain
future tense until their evidence IDs resolve and the campaign is regenerated.

## Channel scope

Authorized for this release:

- LinkedIn personal profile;
- X personal profile;
- GitHub repositories, releases, and the NodeKit launch PR.

Conditional after visual/export proof:

- LinkedIn PDF carousel and native video;
- X architecture image and native video.

Out of scope without a new decision:

- direct messages or automated replies;
- paid promotion;
- automated likes, votes, follows, or comments;
- Reddit, Hacker News, Devpost, Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube publication;
- tagging people or organizations not named in the exact approved copy.

## Final publication gate

Before any final publish click, the campaign receipt must contain:

1. exact text and SHA-256 hash for each channel;
2. exact asset filenames and hashes;
3. the account name visible in the browser;
4. verified working public links;
5. a secret scan with zero findings;
6. the Founder Quest production-proof receipt;
7. the NodeSlide export/reopen receipt;
8. the Feature Proof Studio video receipt.

After publication, record the public URL, timestamp, screenshot hash, and the
rendered text/asset comparison. A draft preview is not a publication receipt.
